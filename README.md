# 🎬 AI News Video Generation Agent

![Python](https://img.shields.io/badge/Python-3.9+-blue)
![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-green)

**An intelligent AI-powered news video automation system that fetches news from domestic and international sources, generates natural emotionally-expressive narration using TTS, and automatically produces professional news videos with real news images.**

## ✨ Core Features

### 🌍 Multi-Source News Aggregation
- Domestic News: Sina News, QQ News, RSS Feeds
- International News: NewsAPI, Hacker News
- Smart Classification: AI & Tech, Manufacturing, Finance
- Auto-detection: Chinese and English

### 🎤 Emotional Voice Synthesis
- Natural Voices: ElevenLabs AI-powered emotional speech
- Multiple Styles: Warm, enthusiastic, calm, professional
- Bilingual Support: Chinese and English
- Dynamic Expression: Intelligent pauses and emphasis

### 📸 Real Image Processing
- Smart Extraction: Real news images only (OG:Image priority)
- Quality Verification: Automatic validation
- No AI Generation: Uses only authentic news images
- Deduplication: Automatic removal of duplicates

### 🎬 Automatic Video Generation
- Dynamic Transitions: Automatic image switching
- Smart Sync: Audio and image auto-synchronization
- HD Output: 1080P and higher support
- Auto Subtitles: Title and content subtitles
